About

Sapling is a native iOS speed reading app that helps you get through books faster without losing comprehension. Import your own PDFs, EPUBs, or plain text files and read at your own pace.

Features

  • ⚡RSVP speed reading with word-by-word display
  • 📖Scrolling reader with text highlighting
  • 🔊Text-to-speech with lookahead buffering
  • 📄Supports PDF, EPUB, and plain text
  • 🔖Per-page bookmarks and reading progress
  • 🎨Themes, fonts, and WPM presets
